FAQ

How do I begin requesting or joining rides?

If you are a Princeton student, begin by simply logging in with your netid. Then, choose the category of your ride to view currently open requests. If you see one you like, join the ride. If not, click on "Add New Rideshare" to make your own. You will receive email confirmations for any rides you join or create. Click here for a more detailed tutorial.

How do I get to my destination?

We leave that up to you and your group! You will all be put in an email chain, and you can click "Reply All" to coordinate the details of your trip.

I don't want to be in a ride anymore. How do I drop it?

If you go to your My Rides page (see the navigation bar), you can see all the rides you are currently in and drop accordingly. You will receive an email confirming you have dropped a ride.

What if I don't see a destination I want to go to in the Airport and Shopping drop down options?

We have an Other category for rides. On the Other requests page, you can make requests for custom locations that aren't currently options in the Airport or Shopping lists, or even destinations that don't fall under those two categories (such as NYC or Philadelphia).

Why should I use Princeton Go?

Princeton Go is a web application that will help you search for rideshares with other Princeton students. This means that you don't have to make list serv email blasts that may or may not actually work trying to find a carpool to the airport. On top of that, carpooling to the airport can actually cut your costs by as much as half or a third, compared to NJ Transit! And for students who are independent, you can find others to go grocery shopping with you so you won't have to rely on Saturday or Sunday shoppers. Will you be offering this service outside of Princeton?

Going into this project, we wanted to grant Princeton students the convenience and comfort of sharing rides with only other Princeton students. As our app takes off at Princeton, we definitely hope to offer local versions for fans of our service elsewhere!

How can I get involved with this project?

Contact any one of the team at our @princeton.edu email (see the Team page under the About drop down) or fill out our feedback form, and we'll get back to you soon!
